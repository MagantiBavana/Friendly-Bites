About Us

We’re a group of passionate friends brought together by one sweet thing - our love for cakes! What started as casual weekend baking sessions quickly turned into a dream we couldn’t ignore. From experimenting with flavors to perfecting frosting swirls, we realized that baking wasn’t just a hobby - it was our calling.

With hearts full of creativity and ovens full of treats, we decided to turn our passion into something bigger - a bakery where every cake tells a story. Whether it’s a birthday, a celebration, or just a craving for something delicious, we’re here to make every moment a little sweeter.

At our bakery, it’s not just about baking - it’s about sharing joy, one slice at a time.
